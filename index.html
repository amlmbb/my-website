Our brand

Our brand aims to sell swimwear to all body types, in all colors and shapes. Our swimsuits are handmade in France, and are shipped to you for a minimum price. We stand for body positivity and our goal is to find the perfect swimsuit for you.

Thanks to our new fabric technology, our swimwears are extremely comfortable and wearable on any occasion, whether you like laying in the sun or diving in the ocean.
